Build notes

  • Form factor = Live Music card. Same 72pt Section row, 56×56 cover (cornerRadius 10), title / author / reason, play.circle.fill. New BookForYouSection placed directly above LiveMusicSection in ListenView.
  • One book, daily. BookForYouStore mirrors LiveMusicOnThisDayStore, cached by YYYY-MM-DD — stable all day, regenerates tomorrow.
  • Personalized. Pool from the listener's audiobook authors & genres → collection:librivoxaudio AND creator:"…" / subject:"…" (reuses the spoken taste profile).
  • Cold start. No history → random from the top 100 subject:"General Fiction" LibriVox titles by downloads.
  • Never a book you've heard. Durable book_listen_history (work-level, survives track eviction) is excluded.
  • Never repeats. Every pick is written to book_curated_history permanently and excluded forever — the never-repeat ledger, not just last-pick like Live Music.
  • Never empty. If exclusion drains the pool, widen: authors+genres → top-100 fiction → broader LibriVox → least-recently-shown.
  • Work-level identity. Hearing one recording of a title blocks recommending another version of it.
