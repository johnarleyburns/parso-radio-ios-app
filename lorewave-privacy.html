Privacy Policy

Lorewave — Effective Date: June 5, 2026

Parso Consulting ("we," "our," or "us") operates the mobile application Lorewave (the "App"), available on the Apple App Store. This Privacy Policy describes our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of information in compliance with App Store requirements and applicable privacy laws.

1. Information We Collect

Lorewave is designed to be privacy-respecting. We collect minimal data, and only where necessary for core functionality:

  • Listening History (on-device only): The App stores a history of tracks you play locally on your device to power personalized "For You" recommendation channels and the Recently Played list. This data never leaves your device.
  • Playlists (on-device only): Any playlists you create are stored locally on your device.
  • Your PIN (on-device only): If you enable Kids Mode, a 4-digit parental PIN is stored locally on your device to prevent unauthorized switching out of Kids Mode.
  • Preferences (on-device only): App appearance (Light/Dark/System), shuffle/repeat settings, and your favorite channels are stored locally.
  • Age Range (Apple-provided): For age assurance, we query Apple's Declared Age Range API to determine whether a user falls into a child (<13), teen (13–17), or adult (18+) bracket. We receive only a bracket — never a birthdate. This bracket is stored locally on your device to configure appropriate content access.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

  • No account or login is required.
  • No personal identifiers (name, email, phone number) are collected.
  • No location data is collected.
  • No usage analytics, tracking, or telemetry is collected.
  • No third-party advertising or ad SDKs are used.
  • No device fingerprinting or cross-app tracking.

3. Audio Content & Streaming

Lorewave streams audio content from public-domain and Creative Commons sources via the Internet Archive, Free Music Archive, and other open repositories. When you play a track, your device makes a direct network request to the source server (e.g., archive.org). We do not proxy, log, or monitor your streaming activity.

Lorewave caches a limited amount of recently played audio on your device to reduce network usage and enable offline playback of previously played tracks. This cache is managed by iOS and can be cleared from within the App's Settings screen.

4. In-App Purchases

Lorewave offers optional in-app contributions (one-time tips and auto-renewable subscriptions) to support the App's hosting, copyright/DMCA handling, and development. All purchases are processed through Apple's StoreKit 2 framework. We do not receive or store your payment card details — Apple handles the entire transaction and we only receive a notification that a purchase completed. Apple's Privacy Policy governs the payment processing.

5. Age Assurance & Kids Mode

Lorewave uses Apple's Declared Age Range API on first launch to determine the appropriate content experience:

  • Children under 13: Automatically restricted to Kids Mode — a curated set of children's songs and stories sourced from the public domain. The main literature catalog, search, news, and in-app purchases are unavailable.
  • Teens 13–17: Full access to the classic literature catalog. All analytics/tracking are automatically disabled as required by state-level minor-protection laws (e.g., Texas SB 2420). The user may optionally switch into Kids Mode from Settings.
  • Adults 18+: Full access to all features and catalog.
  • Fallback (Age unknown): If age information is declined or unavailable, the App defaults to Kids Mode for safety. A parental-gated option to upgrade is available.

6. Content Rights & DMCA

Lorewave indexes and streams only public domain and Creative Commons CC0 content. We do not host any audio files ourselves — all content is sourced from open repositories (primarily the Internet Archive at archive.org). We respect intellectual property rights. If you believe specific content reachable through the App infringes your copyright, please review our DMCA/Copyright procedure in the App's About section, or contact our designated copyright agent at:

  • Email: info@parso.guru (subject: "DMCA / Copyright Report")
  • Copyright agent: Parso Consulting

We aim to acknowledge valid notices within a few business days.

7. Data Sharing

We do not share any information with third parties because we do not collect any. There are no advertising networks, analytics providers, or data brokers integrated into the App.

8. Third-Party Services

The App does not use third-party services (such as analytics, advertising SDKs, or external APIs) that collect user data. Audio is streamed directly from open repositories; these connections are initiated by your device and we do not intermediate or observe them.

9. Data Security

Because we do not collect or store personal data on servers, there is no risk of remote data exposure through the App. All local data (listening history, playlists, preferences) is stored in a SQLite database within the App's sandboxed container, protected by iOS's file-system encryption. You can clear all local data from within the App's Settings screen at any time.

10. Children's Privacy

Lorewave takes children's privacy seriously:

  • For users identified as under 13 via Apple's Declared Age Range API, the App operates exclusively in Kids Mode, which provides only curated children's content from the public domain and collects no personal information whatsoever.
  • We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. No account creation, email collection, or communication channels are available in Kids Mode.
  • Parents can also manually enable Kids Mode from Settings at any time for additional peace of mind.

11. Apple App Store Compliance

In accordance with Apple App Store guidelines:

  • The App does not collect data as defined under Apple's App Privacy requirements beyond the on-device local data described above.
  • The App does not track users across apps or websites — no App Tracking Transparency (ATT) permission dialog is required.
  • The App does not use cookies or similar tracking technologies.
  • The App uses no third-party SDKs beyond the Apple-provided frameworks (StoreKit 2, AVFoundation, Foundation, SwiftUI).
